As your application may handle many accounts or addresses, the IOTA SDK provides a convenient function to list
all of the [`Accounts`](../../references/nodejs/classes/Account/) related to a wallet,
[`wallet.getAccounts()`](../../references/nodejs/classes/Wallet/#getaccounts).

This allows you to provide accurate and up-to-date information to your users, such as the account name, balance,
transaction history, and other relevant metadata, facilitating seamless management of multiple accounts within your
platform.
