When you burn an [NFT](https://wiki.iota.org/shimmer/develop/explanations/what-is-stardust/tokenization/#non-fungible-tokens-nfts),
you permanently remove it from the ledger state, rendering it unusable and untransferable. As you need to
place a [storage deposit](https://wiki.iota.org/shimmer/develop/explanations/what-is-stardust/storage-deposit/) for any
NFT, the deposit will automatically be returned to its original account.

:::warning irreversible

Burning an NFT is irreversible and signifies the destruction of its digital representation and
associated ownership rights.

:::
